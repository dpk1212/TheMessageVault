Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really anonymous?

+

Yes, completely anonymous until you choose to reveal yourself. We use enterprise-grade encryption and privacy measures to ensure your identity remains hidden. Recipients will receive messages from a neutral phone number or email address that cannot be traced back to you.

How are messages delivered?

+

Messages are delivered via text or email (recipient's choice). They'll receive one message per day at a time you select. Our delivery system ensures timely arrival and tracks when messages are opened without revealing your identity.

What if I want to stop sending messages?

+

You can pause or stop your messages at any time from your secure dashboard. Your recipient will never know who started or stopped the messages unless you choose to reveal yourself. You can also schedule a specific end date for your message series.

Can recipients reply to messages?

+

Yes, recipients can reply to messages. You'll receive their replies in your dashboard while maintaining your anonymity. This creates a secure two-way communication channel that protects your identity until you're ready to reveal yourself.

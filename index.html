Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really anonymous?

+

Yes, completely anonymous until you choose to reveal yourself. Recipients receive messages from our system with no way to identify who sent them unless you decide to share your identity.

How are messages delivered?

+

Messages are delivered via text message or email (recipient's preference). They'll receive messages at varying times on the schedule you select - weekly, three times a week, or daily.

How do I reveal myself as the sender?

+

When you're ready to reveal your identity, you can do so with a single click through your account dashboard. Your recipient will receive a special message letting them know you've been the one sending the encouragement all along.

Can I write my own messages?

+

Yes! While we have a large library of uplifting messages, you can also customize and create your own. The Medium and Heavy plans include theme options like mindfulness, personal growth, and motivation to help you craft the perfect message.

Can recipients reply to messages?

+

Yes, recipients can reply to messages. Their responses will be delivered to your dashboard while maintaining your anonymity, allowing for meaningful two-way communication.
